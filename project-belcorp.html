Overview

The Challenge

Belcorp is one of Latin America's most influential beauty companies, operating L'Bel, Cyzone, and Ésika across 15 countries. Their e-commerce channel was growing but faced a critical gap: customers had no personalised guidance in the digital purchase journey — the kind of expert advice that their field sales advisors provided in person.

We were challenged to design an AI-powered solution that could replicate this personalised experience at scale — improving customer experience, increasing conversion, and capturing valuable purchase behaviour data.

The problem: Belcorp's e-commerce lacked the personalisation that drove offline sales. Customers didn't receive skin-type guidance, advisor connections, or tailored product recommendations — leading to drop-offs in the digital purchase funnel.

Methodology

How We Approached It

01
E-commerce Analysis
Analysed Belcorp's digital sales performance across L'Bel, Cyzone, and Ésika — identifying where customers dropped off and what personalisation gaps existed compared to the in-person advisor model.
02
Stakeholder Research
Interviewed Ésika sellers to understand how they built customer trust and drove conversions face-to-face. Spoke with the Head of Marketing & Analytics to map the data gaps the business most needed to close.

The Solution

Assistant Capabilities

  • Skin Type Profiling: Conversational quiz that identifies customer skin type and concerns, enabling personalised product recommendations
  • Product Recommendations: AI-driven suggestions matched to the customer's profile across L'Bel, Cyzone, and Ésika catalogues
  • Advisor Connection: Seamless handoff to a live Ésika sales advisor for complex queries — preserving the human element of the brand
  • Behavioural Data Capture: Every interaction generates structured data on preferences, purchase intent, and objections — filling a critical gap in Belcorp's analytics
Strategic insight: The most successful feature wasn't the AI itself — it was the advisor handoff. By preserving the human connection at the right moment in the journey, we maintained the trust that made Belcorp's offline sales model so effective.
