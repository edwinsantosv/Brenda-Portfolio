About me

Hello, I'm Brenda

Senior UX Designer and Design Lead with experience leading multidisciplinary teams and delivering UX/UI and product design solutions across corporate and fast-growing global companies.

Based in Lima, Peru and currently at NEORIS. I hold an MSc in Global Strategy & Innovation Management from the University of Leeds (awarded the Master's Prize for Extracurricular Activity, Dec 2023) and an undergraduate degree from Pontificia Universidad Católica del Perú (PUCP).

I combine human-centred design thinking with strategic business vision — running research, facilitating workshops, owning stakeholder relationships, and shipping products that solve real problems. Recognised by NASA, Belcorp, BCP and Co-op (UK).

"My goal is to bridge design and business — leading teams that build products people love and organisations need."

Track the Plastic

A digital platform that detects, monitors and quantifies plastic pollution in ocean coastal areas — using computer vision, NASA satellite imagery, a virtual assistant and NLP. Users can upload contaminated area images, access a global heat map and view historical pollution data.

My role
Leader of a multidisciplinary team: UX Designers, Data Engineer, AI Scientist, AI Engineer and Software Engineer.
  • Led research, analysis and insights on marine plastic debris
  • Facilitated brainstorming sessions and solution architecture
  • Drove product development and feature definition
  • Prepared and presented the solution deliverable to NASA judges
Result: 2nd place over 1,000+ proposals. Global Finalist Honorable Mention — the only Peruvian project among 57,000+ participants. Recognised by NASA employees and Peruvian national media.
